About Us

Rooted in Hubli. Built on trust.

Ecofin Credit Souharda Sahakari Niyamit is a member-owned cooperative credit society based in Hubli, dedicated to bringing honest, accessible financial services to individuals, families, and small businesses. Every loan we sanction and every deposit we hold is guided by the cooperative spirit of shared ownership, transparency, and mutual growth.

We believe banking should feel personal. That's why our members are not just customers — they are partners in a shared financial future.
